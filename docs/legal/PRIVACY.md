# AI Assassins Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-15

## Data We Collect
- Account identifiers (email, user id)
- Subscription status and billing entitlement metadata
- Usage telemetry (brief count, timestamp, request latency)
- User configuration (focus, tone, optional location and ICS URL)

## Data We Do Not Store
- Payment card details (handled by Apple/Google/RevenueCat)
- Secrets/API keys on the client

## Purpose of Processing
- Authenticate users
- Enforce free-tier quotas and paid-tier entitlements
- Generate and improve briefing reliability

## Legal Basis
- Contract performance (service delivery)
- Legitimate interests (fraud prevention, diagnostics)
- Consent (optional location)

## Retention
- Usage and entitlement records retained for operational and audit requirements
- You may request account deletion and data export where applicable

## Third Parties
- Supabase/Clerk (authentication)
- RevenueCat (subscription entitlement)
- Apple/Google billing platforms
- OpenAI (brief generation)

## Security
- HTTPS in transit
- Server-side secret management
- Least-privilege access controls
