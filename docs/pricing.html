How does billing work?
On web/PWA, checkout is handled securely via Stripe. Your tier syncs back to the app after payment.
Does this work on the App Store version?
If you publish a native iOS/Android wrapper, you typically use in-app purchases for digital subscriptions. We can wire RevenueCat for that while keeping web Stripe for the PWA.
What if I paid but the tier didn’t update?
Open the app, tap “Login”, then “Sync / Refresh”. The Worker checks your subscription status.
